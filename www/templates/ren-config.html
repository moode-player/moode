Renderers

Audio renderers are playback applications separate from MPD. When a client connects to one of the renderers MPD will stop and the renderer will begin playback. When the client stops or disconnects, MPD can resume playback depending on the "Resume MPD" setting.

Bluetooth

A Bluetooth adapter must be present and enabled otherwise Bluetooth will not start.

Bluetooth by Johan Hedberg (BlueZ) and Arkadiusz Bokowy (Bluez-alsa).
$_select[pairing_agent_on] $_select[pairing_agent_off]
Automatically accept incoming pairing and connect requests without having to perform a Bluetooth scan.
$_select[rsmafterbt_yes] $_select[rsmafterbt_no]
Resume MPD playback after the Bluetooth client disconnects.
