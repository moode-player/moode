                    Advanced search

                    Search fields
                    The filters listed below will override "All tags" or "Specific tags" searches. Filter names are case insensative.
                    Encoded [bits | rate | bits/rate | Nch | multichannel]
                    Format [.ext]
                    Folder [path]
                    HDonly Sample rate > 44.1 kHz or bit depth > 16
                    Lossless Formats include AIF|AIFF|ALAC|FLAC|DFF|DSF|WAV
                    Lossy Formats other than Lossless above
                    Year [yyyy] or [yyyy-yyyy]
                    Examples: encoded 24/96 | encoded 192 | format .dsf | folder u2/achtung baby | year 1962 | year 1994-1998
                    All tags are searched using an OR relationship. Searches are not case sensitive and partial matches are the default unless one of the strict comparison operators '==' or '!=' is used. The comparison operators '=~' or '!~' can also be used to perform searches based on a PERL regular expressions. There must be a space between the comparison operator and the search string.
                    Search specific tags
                    Non-empty tags are searched using an AND relationship. Searches are not case sensitive and partial matches are the default unless one of the strict comparison operators '==' or '!=' is used. The comparison operators '=~' or '!~' can also be used to perform searches based on PERL regular expressions. There must be a space between the comparison operator and the search string.
