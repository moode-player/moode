                    Select how to populate the Artist list in Tag view.
                    Artist: List all Artists, Performers and Conductors. Compilation albums listed for a selected artist will show only the tracks belonging to the artist. Clicking the Album will toggle between showing all the album's tracks and just those for the selected artist.
